Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our application ("the App") handles user information.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect or store personal information from users.

The App may receive limited technical information necessary to operate the service, such as device type and operating system version, which is handled by the operating system and app distribution platforms.

2. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional digital items that can be purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play.

All payments are processed directly by the platform provider. The App does not collect, process, or store payment information such as credit card numbers.

3. Tracking

The App does not track users across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

The App does not use Apple's Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) and does not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Security

Since the App does not collect personal data, no personal information is stored on our servers.
